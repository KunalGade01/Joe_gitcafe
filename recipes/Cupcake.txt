Ingredients:
1.5 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup unsalted butter
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
1.5 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup milk,

How to make:
Step1: Mix dry ingredients.
Step2: Cream butter and sugar, add eggs and vanilla.
Step3:Combine wet and dry ingredients, add milk.
STep4:Fill cupcake liners, bake at 350°F (175°C) for 18-20 mins.
