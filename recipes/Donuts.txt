Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp active dry yeast
3/4 cup warm milk
1/4 cup melted butter
2 large eggs
Vegetable oil for frying.

How to make:
Step1: Mix flour, sugar, salt, and yeast.
Step2: Add warm milk, melted butter, and eggs.
Step3: Knead until smooth, rise until doubled.
Step4: Roll, cut, and fry in oil until golden.
