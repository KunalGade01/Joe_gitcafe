Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup sugar
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
Optional: chocolate chips or nuts

How to make:
Step1: Cream butter and sugar, add vanilla.
Step2: Mix in dry ingredients gradually.
Step3: Add chocolate chips or nuts if desired.
Step4: Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet, bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10-12 mins.
